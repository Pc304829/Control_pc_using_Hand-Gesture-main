## 🛠️ How to Use the Software

Once the program opens, a small window will appear. This window shows what your webcam sees. The software tracks your hand and maps it to your cursor position.

### Moving the Mouse
Hold your hand in front of the camera. The software detects your index finger. Move your finger in the air to move the cursor on your screen. Keep your hand within view of the camera lens for the best results.

### Scrolling Down Pages
Open your hand and show all five fingers to the camera. Move your hand up or down to scroll through websites or documents. This gesture mimics the motion of sliding your hand across a glass surface.

### Adjusting System Volume
Make a fist with your hand. Move your fist up to increase the volume. Move your fist down to decrease the volume. The software shows an indicator on your screen to reflect these changes.

## 🛡️ Safety Features

The software includes built-in safeguards to ensure you maintain control of your machine.

* **Kill Switch:** Press the 'Q' key on your keyboard at any time. This stops the program and releases control of your mouse.
* **Auto-Pause:** If you walk away from your desk, the software stops tracking your hand. It resumes once it detects your hand again.
* **Movement Smoothing:** The program filters out jittery movements. This ensures the cursor moves across the screen in a straight and predictable line.

## 📋 System Requirements

Ensure your computer meets these requirements to run the software correctly.

* **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* **Camera:** A standard USB webcam or a built-in laptop camera.
* **Lighting:** A well-lit room helps the software see your hand gestures. Avoid glare or strong light sources, like a window, directly behind you.
* **Processor:** A modern computer processor provides the best tracking speed. 

## 🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the software record my video?**
No. The software analyzes your video feed in real-time but does not save or send images to the internet. 

**What if the cursor jumps around?**
Clean your camera lens. Ensure your hand appears clearly against a simple background. If the room is dark, the software may struggle to track your fingers.

**How do I click items on screen?**
Bring your index finger and thumb together in a pinching motion to register a click. The software acknowledges this gesture as a standard left mouse click.

**Can I use this for playing games?**
This software works for common office tasks and media control. It provides less precision than a standard gaming mouse, so it may not be suitable for high-speed gaming.

**How do I close the program?**
Press the 'Q' key or click the 'X' button on the top right corner of the application window.
